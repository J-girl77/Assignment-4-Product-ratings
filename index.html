Product Ratings Dashboard

This dashboard fetches product data from the Fake Store API and visualizes the ratings in two different ways.

Loading product data…

Average Rating by Category

This column chart shows the average customer rating (from 0 to 5) for each product category. It helps identify which categories tend to have the most highly rated products.

Rating Distribution

This pie chart groups every product by its rating into four ranges (below 3, 3–3.9, 4–4.4, and 4.5 or higher) so you can see how ratings are distributed across the whole catalog.

Data source: Fake Store API. Charts rendered with Highcharts.
